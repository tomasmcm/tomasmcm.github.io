Project Title

WS 10Y

Description

For the celebration of Whitesmith’s 10 years (2012 – 2022), 12 pieces were modelled, 3D printed and minted into NFTs for each of the company stakeholders.


The 3D models are based on a dodecahedron puzzle that was modified to include the WS logo and the number of the piece. Each unique piece was 3D printed on a Creality Ender 3 using golden PLA filament.


On the inside of each piece there is a QR code that points to the NFT for that piece. NFTs were minted on OpenSea (Polygon) using 3D renders from Fusion360.
