Project Title

Mini Untz

Description

The Mini Untz is a small version of the Arduinome with the added funcionality of the four potentiometers. Besides de 4 by 4 grid of silicone buttons that output basic MIDI commands, the potentiometers are bind to special MIDI control change commands to manipulate volume, modulation, balance, or pan.

Arduino, Processing, Ableton
