Project Title

regenMeUp

Description

During ETHPorto 2023’s Hackathon we built a DAPP where Regen Punks can participate by attesting fellow punks on their work in real world activities to fight climate change. Once a punk gathers enough votes they can mint a badge that allows them to receive a regen basic income paid in BCT (Base Carbon Tonnes) based on their reputation.


Decentralised Regen Punk army fighting Climate Change

We created the base smart contracts, dapp and Chainlink upkeeps to call the drip method everyday. The live dapp is available on regenmeup.on.fleek.co (Polygon Mumbai). The project submission page can be viewed on Taikai.

(This project was done along side Rafael Jegundo, Charles Forth and Maria Ferreira).
